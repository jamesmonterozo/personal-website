Hello! I'm Clarence.

I am an incoming 1st Year BSMS CS student in the Ateneo de Manila University who loves learning, research, leadership, and community involvement.

As someone who enjoys research and data science, I am currently honing my skills in Data Science as a scholar of the DOST-SPARTA Project and The Coding School's Data Science Scholarship in partnership with Data Camp, both under the Data Scientist track. I am also looking for internship opportunities in Data Science or Research.

I am passionate about service and involvement in my community, and I have led teams of passionate people in several school organizations over the past six (6) years. Currently, I am the founder of the Data Analytics Committee, a new committee that aims to encourage the use of data analytics in school organizations. I am also the Chairperson of the Commission on Elections, and I led the organization of the first set of fully online elections in the ASHS. Recently, I joined as a Student Volunteer in the ADMU's Office of the Vice President for Social Development to assist in the OVPSD's Voter Registration initiatives. As the Co-Head for Institutional Coordination, I am in charge of liaising between the OVPSD and different institutions on voter registration initiatives.

As someone who loves learning, I am hoping to spend my summer improving on my technical skills and hopefully interning in a data science or research-related position. I am open to all opportunities regardless of compensation because I am mainly after the learning experience and contributing to the organization.
